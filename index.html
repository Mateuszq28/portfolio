Chaszcze QR INO

Android application for touchless orienteering march

My friend was wondering how to speed up the process of checking the cards of orienteering participants (INO) and at the same time meet covid-19 restrictions. We together agreed that the best solution would be a smartphone application that uses a camera to scan QR codes. It was a great substitute for colored pencils that would be touched by all participants to write down codes from lanterns hidden in the forest. Since my friend has no knowledge of programming, I wrote the application and he organized the sporting event.
