I am a student and an entrepreneur. In 2021 I graduated from the field of Electronics and Telecommunications (Telecommunication Stream, specialty: Multimedia Systems), obtaining a bachelor degree. In my Engineering Thesis I wrote and described software for spatial filtering of sound in the frequency domain. In 2021-2022 I studied Biomedical Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence. I participated in the government AI-TECH project related to Machine Learning. Together with a three-person team, in cooperation with the Invicta infertility clinic, we dealt with the segmentation and detection of sperm abnormalities in microscopic images. Our work culminated in a publication, published in the IEEE database and presented at a Human System Interaction 2022 conference in Melbourne, Australia. I also attended the 26th International Conference on Pattern Recognition in Montreal, Canada. For the last 1.5 years I have been working on my own online store and a few side projects. Currently, I am in the process of describing the results of my research on Monte Carlo light simulation as a part of my master's thesis.

I dream of working with artificial intelligence in computer games in the future, especially with those ones created in Unreal Engine. I also follow the development of quantum computers with interest and plan to learn more about quantum algorithms.

Chaszcze QR INO

Android application for touchless orienteering march

My friend was wondering how to speed up the process of checking the cards of orienteering participants (INO) and at the same time meet covid-19 restrictions. We together agreed that the best solution would be a smartphone application that uses a camera to scan QR codes. It was a great substitute for colored pencils that would be touched by all participants to write down codes from lanterns hidden in the forest. Since my friend has no knowledge of programming, I wrote the application and he organized the sporting event.
